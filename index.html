About Me

Hi, I'm Prathap, founder of Mani Chakra Gold Fin. With over 10 years of experience in the finance and gold loan industry, I’ve worked with multiple reputed institutions and gained deep knowledge of how the system works — and more importantly, how it can work better for you.

After years in the field, I realized that many customers are stuck with high-interest gold loans and don’t even know they have better options. I saw people struggling to shift their gold from one lender to another simply because of a lack of money or proper guidance. That’s when the idea of Mani Chakra Gold Fin was born — and I decided to act on it immediately.

Our mission is simple: to help people break free from high-interest loans, guide them to the best banks offering low interest rates and high gram value, and assist them through the entire process — including arranging takeover amounts and handling all the documentation.

At Mani Chakra Gold Fin, we don’t just offer gold loan services. We offer peace of mind, freedom from high interest, and a chance to get more out of your gold — because every gram matters.
